Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment Analysis is used here to identify and label articles and documents based off of certain word meanings.
We label articles as objective, subjective or neutral based off of these specific words and give them different weightings. For example:
"In my opinion" is more subjective, while "Research shows" is more objective.

Additionally we also label articles either positive, negative or neutral polarity of how it looks at its subject.
This is done exactly the same as above, just different weightings of words.
